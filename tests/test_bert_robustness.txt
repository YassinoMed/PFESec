[TEST-ROB-003] Email très long légitime
---
TEXT: Dear Team, I hope this email finds you well. Following our quarterly security review meeting held on May 28, 2026, I wanted to share the comprehensive findings from our penetration testing engagement conducted by CrowdStrike. The assessment covered our entire external perimeter including 47 public-facing applications, 12 API endpoints, and our VPN infrastructure. Key findings include: 1) Two medium-severity cross-site scripting vulnerabilities in the customer portal (already patched as of June 1), 2) One low-severity information disclosure via verbose error messages in the staging environment, 3) Certificate pinning bypass on the mobile application (Android only), 4) Outdated TLS 1.0 support on legacy load balancer LB-LEGACY-02. All critical and high-severity findings from the previous quarter have been remediated and verified. The full 87-page report is available on our SharePoint security folder. Next steps: We will schedule individual remediation meetings with each application owner by end of week. Please review the findings relevant to your systems before then. If you have any questions, please reach out to the security team at security@company.com or via our Slack channel #security-ops. Best regards, Michael Chen, Director of Information Security.
---
LABEL_ATTENDU: Safe Email
TYPE: Email long légitime — test de contexte

[TEST-ADV-001] Injection de contexte légitime dans phishing
---
TEXT: Security Bulletin - Microsoft Security Response Center
Following our regular Patch Tuesday cycle, we are issuing an emergency out-of-band security update. A critical zero-day vulnerability (CVE-2026-99999) affects all versions of Windows. 
Download the emergency patch immediately: http://microsoft-emergency-patch.com/download
This update addresses a remote code execution vulnerability being actively exploited in the wild.
Microsoft Corporation, One Microsoft Way, Redmond, WA 98052
---
LABEL_ATTENDU: Phishing Email
TYPE: Phishing camouflé en bulletin sécurité Microsoft — adversarial

[TEST-ADV-002] Email légitime avec mots-clés phishing
---
TEXT: Dear Security Team, During our analysis of the latest phishing campaign targeting our employees, we identified the following characteristics: the attackers are using urgent language like "your account will be suspended", asking users to "click here to verify", and spoofing our domain with "company-secure.com". We've updated our email filters and blocked 847 phishing emails. Please remind your teams to report suspicious emails to phishing@company.com. Attached is the full IOC list for your review.
---
LABEL_ATTENDU: Safe Email
TYPE: Email légitime parlant DE phishing — adversarial (false positive trap)

[TEST-ADV-003] Phishing avec disclaimer légal
---
TEXT: IMPORTANT NOTICE: This email and any attachments are confidential and intended solely for the recipient. If you received this in error, please delete it.

Dear Customer,
We detected suspicious login activity on your account from IP 185.220.101.45 (Moscow, Russia). 
For your security, we have temporarily locked your account.
Restore access now: https://secure-login.bank-verify.xyz/restore
This action is required within 24 hours to avoid permanent suspension.

This communication is from the Fraud Prevention Department. Do not reply to this email.
Bank Security Operations | Reg. No. 12345678
---
LABEL_ATTENDU: Phishing Email
TYPE: Phishing avec éléments de légitimité (disclaimer, reg. number) — adversarial

[TEST-ADV-004] Email neutre - ni phishing ni vraiment safe
---
TEXT: Hello, just checking in about the project status. Can you send me the updated spreadsheet when you get a chance? Thanks.
---
LABEL_ATTENDU: Safe Email
TYPE: Email anodin neutre — test de calibration

[TEST-ADV-005] Spear phishing ultra-ciblé
---
TEXT: Hi Sarah, Great catching up with you at the RSA Conference last week! As promised, here's the threat intelligence report we discussed about the APT29 campaign targeting the financial sector. I've uploaded it to our shared folder: https://sharepoint-company.onedrive-secure.xyz/shared/APT29_TI_Report.pdf. Let me know your thoughts on the attribution analysis in section 3. Best, Dave (CrowdStrike)
---
LABEL_ATTENDU: Phishing Email
TYPE: Spear phishing personnalisé avec contexte réel — très difficile à détecter
